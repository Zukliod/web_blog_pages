Part IV: Strategic Recommendations & Roadmap

A phased approach to avoid the pitfalls of rigidity.

A Phased Development Approach

Phase 1: MVP - The AI Co-Pilot

Focus on data ingestion and a Natural Language Query interface for immediate value in reporting and analysis.

Phase 2: Predictive Functionality

Build the forecasting engine with core ML models and integration of external data sources.

Phase 3: Prescriptive & Proactive

Integrate a prescriptive layer with actionable recommendations and market simulation capabilities.

Phase 1: Minimum Viable Product (MVP) - The AI Co-Pilot for Reporting.

The initial focus will be on building a robust data ingestion layer and the generative AI-powered NLQ interface. The MVP will allow users to connect to existing data sources and use natural language to ask questions, generate reports, and create visualizations. This provides immediate value by solving the most acute pain points of manual effort and data overload while building user confidence and adoption. This approach addresses the organizational challenge of resistance to change by demonstrating clear, immediate benefits.
